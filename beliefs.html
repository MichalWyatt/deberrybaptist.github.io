What We Believe

The Bible

“All Scripture is God-breathed…”
— 2 Timothy 3:16

Every word is perfect, inerrant, and our final authority.

Salvation

“Believe on the Lord Jesus Christ…”
— Acts 16:31

By grace alone, through faith alone, in Christ alone.

The Trinity

“Father, Son, and Holy Spirit…”
— Matthew 28:19

One God in three Persons—eternally co-equal.

Baptism & Communion

“Go therefore and make disciples…”
— Matthew 28:19

Believer’s baptism by immersion • Lord’s Supper as remembrance.
